## **MARKET SNAPSHOT REQUIREMENTS (both editions)**

**3-Card Grid (top):**

* **System Peak Load (MW)** — latest **24h** or most recent official; include exact value + "as of {HH\:MM Africa/Cairo, DD Mon YYYY}"; **EEHC/EETC** link.
* **Curtailment Status** — "None" or total **hours** in last **24h/7d** (state period); source: **MoERE/EEHC/EETC**.
* **RE Share or Capacity** — latest **% of generation** (monthly) **or** cumulative **installed RE (MW)** (NREA/EgyptERA). Include period + link.

**Tariff Mini-Tables (directly under cards):**

* **Residential Tariffs (EGP/kWh)** — **Slab** | **kWh Range** | **Price (EGP/kWh)** | **Notes** (lifeline, planned deferrals).
* **Industrial/Commercial (EGP/kWh)** — **Voltage Level (MV/HV/EHV)** | **TOU Band (Peak/Off-peak/Shoulder if applicable)** | **Price (EGP/kWh)** | **Notes** (subsidy/rebate/policy date).

*(If any metric lacks fresh official data: write **"insufficient data"** and skip — never estimate.)*
